About King Hi-Tech

King Hi-Tech (Hong Kong) Ship Machine Equipment Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive enterprise specializing in the supply of marine diesel engine spare parts, core component manufacturing, and professional repair services.

Supported by two strategic warehouses in Dalian and Shanghai, we have established an efficient supply chain network covering major ports across northern and southern China. Guided by our service philosophy of "Professional Assurance, Mission Accomplished," we provide timely and reliable maritime solutions to global clients.

Leveraging deep industry expertise, we have established solid partnerships with several leading domestic diesel engine manufacturers. As an OEM-approved supplier of key components, we deliver piston rods, piston heads, and other core parts manufactured to original standards, alongside round-the-clock maintenance support.

Our company has assembled a professional technical service team and maintains close collaboration with most OEMs on after-sales support. Backed by our dual-warehouse inventory system and extensive industry experience, we can respond promptly to shipowners' requirements, supply parts quickly, and provide precise and efficient technical support.
