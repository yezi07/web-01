About King Hitech

Founded in 2003, King Hitech Limited has established itself as a premier provider of comprehensive maritime services. Our journey began with a simple mission: to deliver reliable, high-quality solutions to the global shipping industry.

Over the years, we've grown from a small ship supply company to a full-service maritime solutions provider with a global footprint. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of ship owners and operators worldwide.

Today, we operate from strategic locations across Asia, Europe, and the Americas, ensuring that our clients receive timely support wherever their vessels may be. Our team of over 200 dedicated professionals brings deep industry expertise to every project.

At King Hitech, we believe that success is built on strong relationships, technical expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality. These principles guide everything we do, from sourcing the right spare parts to executing complex repair projects.
