Our Services

We offer comprehensive maritime solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of ship owners and operators worldwide.

Engine Component Manufacturing & Parts Supply

We professionally produce piston rods, piston heads, and core fuel/exhaust system components as authorized by licensed manufacturers and original factory parts. We strictly adhere to original factory standards and specifications, ensuring that every product delivers excellent performance and reliable quality, making it a trustworthy choice.

Our strategically located warehouses in Dalian (North) and Shanghai (South) allow us to efficiently manage and distribute products globally, ensuring timely order delivery and consistent high-quality service.

  • Piston rods and piston heads manufacturing
  • Core fuel/exhaust system components
  • Authorized licensed & genuine parts
  • Strategic warehouse locations

Maintenance Services

King Hi-Tech's global maintenance and engineering team is committed to providing professional, efficient, and reliable technical services for marine power systems. Our core services include:

  • Main/auxiliary engine maintenance
  • 5-year major overhauls
  • Electronic fuel injection engine repair (ME Engines, FLEX Engines)
  • 24/7 emergency troubleshooting

Our service team consists of experienced technicians and professional assemblers with solid engineering capabilities and extensive field experience. We are committed to delivering high-quality, short-cycle, and cost-effective solutions.

Parts Inventory System
